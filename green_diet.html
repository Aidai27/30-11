About Project

Green Diet – is a service for ordering fast food delivery from your favorite restaurants via mobile app. On average, we deliver food in 34 minutes after placing an order. We carefully select restaurants and try to provide a variety of dishes.

Research

I started by researching various food delivery apps ( Uber Eats, Doordash, Glovo, and GrubHub ) and reading clients’ feedback. I selected important features for each app and studied reviews to identify the poor areas and what features users need. It helps to understand what users are experiencing while using the apps and to prepare the user interview questions.

  • Provide the contact information for the deliverer and the restaurants.
  • Put the exact delivery time.
  • If the delivery is going to be late, the customer must be alerted.
  • An online map allows you to track the order and delivery.
  • Reduce unnecessary notifications.
  • Contact the delivery person directly (in case if the food is lost, or late).
  • Make the option to save your favorite food or restaurant

Problem Solving

I provide a faster way to order, schedule, and deliver food with simple functions.

I created this food delivery app with a user-centered design to improve the user’s experience.
